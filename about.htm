About Kelly Tours and Travels

Kelly Tours and Travels has been in business as a full-service travel agency since 2009. We have a team of travel specialists who are renowned for providing personalized service for all your corporate and leisure travel needs.

Our agents are veterans at corporate travel and have traveled the globe gathering information, details and suggestions to truly tailor a vacation, wedding or honeymoon to meet your needs and preferences.
