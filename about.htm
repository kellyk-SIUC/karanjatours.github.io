About Karanja Tours and Travels

Karanja Tours and Travels has been in business as a full-service travel agency since 2009. We have a team of travel specialists who are renowned for providing personalized service for all your corporate and leisure travel needs.
Our agents are veterans at corporate travel and have traveled the globe gathering information, details and suggestions to truly tailor a vacation, wedding or honeymoon to meet your needs and preferences.

Why use a Travel Agent?

There are a ton of reasons to use a travel agent, but it boils down to:

>The cost is free to you.
>Travel advisor works for you and your family.
>Travel advisor shares knowledge and helps you make the best decision.
>And most importantly, your travel advisor will find you the lowest price.
